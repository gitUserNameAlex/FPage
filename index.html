Lil Mosey (Lathan Moses Echols) is an American rapper, songwriter

and singer. He was born January 25, 2002 in Seattle, Washington.

His most commercially successful song, "Noticed", peaked at number 80

on the Billboard Hot 100. Lil Mosey is known for his melodic flow and lyrics

that are tailored to his life. When asked if the term "mumble rap" applies to him

in an interview with Complex, Lil Mosey answered: "I wouldn’t consider myself a

mumble rapper, because I don’t know what that is. But when I talk, I mumble."
